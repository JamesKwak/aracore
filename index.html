What we solve

Fast payout is easy. Safe settlement is the hard part.

Aracore separates customer payout from institutional settlement, then connects them with lock verification, quote messaging, evidence tracking, and net settlement orchestration.

Legacy cross-border flow

×Capital is pre-funded across multiple corridors.
×Settlement visibility is fragmented across banks and intermediaries.
×Local payout and final settlement are difficult to reconcile in real time.
×Liquidity cost, operational cost, and partner exposure increase as volume grows.
→

Aracore ISN

✓Lock-based assurance before local payout.
✓Quote, payout, evidence, and settlement states are orchestrated in one workflow.
✓Net settlement reduces the cross-border amount that must move.
✓Banks stay in control of customers, compliance, and settlement decisions.
