About Aracore

Aracore builds infrastructure for cross-border payout and settlement between financial institutions. Our goal is to make payout execution and final settlement easier to coordinate, audit, and scale.

Mission

Connect institutional payout and settlement flows in a safer and simpler operating model.


Vision

Build the network layer that lets regulated financial participants settle value across digital dollar systems.
