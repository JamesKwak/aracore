Use Cases

Use cases for institutions that need controlled settlement flows.

Cross-border payout is covered on the developers page. These scenarios show how the ISN handles on/off ramp conversion and network liquidity as separate but connected steps.

Live Scenario

Cross-border payout

Payout requests, quotes, payout approval, local execution, and final settlement move through one reference. The ISN routes secure messages between institutions and locks collateral on-chain, so funds only release once both sides are validated.

On / Off ramp

An institution at the edge of a corridor needs to convert stablecoin into local fiat currency. The ISN routes the request to a licensed third-party partner in that country. Korea and Thailand are operated directly by the Aracore network, while other countries are served through partner operators.

Future Service

Liquidity supply

A corridor participant is facing a large settlement but does not hold enough stablecoin. The participant connects to the ISN liquidity network, borrows as much as needed, and pays interest and fees. Without finding and negotiating with individual providers directly, a single ISN connection gives access to liquidity across the whole network.
